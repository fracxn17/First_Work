🆘 Help Center

How to Ask a Doubt?

Go to the Dashboard → Click on "Ask a Doubt" → Describe your problem clearly. You’ll get connected with a peer in real time.

How to Host a Class?

On the dashboard, select “Host a Class”. Fill in your topic, date, and time. Learners can then join your session live.

How to Earn Credits?

  • Answer doubts in the Doubt Room
  • Host sessions and get rated
  • Invite friends using your referral code

Having Login or Account Issues?

Try clearing your browser cache or resetting your password. If the issue continues, contact our support at support@looplearn.in.
